Starbucks unionization : a long way ahead

While Starbucks has entered negotiations with over 200 stores, several outlets efforts to unionize were unsuccessful.

By Mark Banchereau | December 18th, 2022

Starbucks signboard. Photo by Dom J (free of use on Pixels)

In December 2021, a Starbucks location in the Buffalo area won a union election, the first time since the 1980s. A unionization sweep quickly spread throughout the United States. According to the National Labor Relations Board, at least 257 Starbucks locations have voted to unionize since late 2021. So far, Starbucks has entered contract negotiations with at least 53 stores, with 13 more sessions scheduled later in December.
